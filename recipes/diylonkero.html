DIY lonkero

Description

This is a strictly Finnish drink made by combining cheap gin with citrus. The most common flavor is grapefruit, which is reproduced here.

Lonkero is not sold in the US so I decided to try to copy it by referring to web sites from other tourists who tried to copy it and reading the ingredients on the company's web site.

Ingredients

  • 4 ice cubes
  • 2 tablespoons gin, or to taste
  • â…” cup grapefruit juice, or to taste
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1 tablespoon lime juice
  • 1 cup club soda

Steps

  1. Place ice cubes in a cocktail glass. Pour gin over ice cubes; add grapefruit juice, lemon juice, and lime juice. Top with club soda and stir gently.
